About us

InsideOut started with the idea of bringing together the diverse know-how and skills of security professionals coming from heterogeneous backgrounds: from corporate, to the field of the internet, to top level universities. This allows InsideOut to offer top-quality Cyber-security services and consultancy to critical customers ranging from the Finance sector to large utilities and engineering firms. During the past years, we successfully accomplished cyber operations such as security analyses of complex corporate systems and the creation of software and tools that enabled our customers to improve their cyber security posture ever more.
